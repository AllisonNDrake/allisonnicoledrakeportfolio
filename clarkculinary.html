Culinary Arts at Clark College

Web Video Production

With the help of a fellow student, this film was done with the intention to explore the Tod and Maxine McClaskey Culinary Institute at Clark College in Vancouver, WA. I interviewed two students who discussed their time at Clark in the Culinary Management and Baking and Pastry Arts programs.
